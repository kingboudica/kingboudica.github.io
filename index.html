Thornberry

Koemon

Koemon is a Beast Digimon that appears to be a baby ape and is deceptively strong for its size. It carries a slingshot which it shoots with incredible accuracy for its Special Move.

Stats

Agility: 4
Heart: 2
Brains: 2
Power: 4

Special Moves

Spit Wad
Thornberry removes emails from his messenger bag, sticks them in his mouth and chews them up.
Once they're nice and disgusting, he makes an electrically charged spit wad that he fires from his sling shot with remarkable accuracy.
Options:
Sure Strike: on a miss, attack does half damage automatically.
Poison: forgoes damage, instead deals damage equal to Agility at the end of each of the target's turns until the end of the combat.
